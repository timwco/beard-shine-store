Q: Why Beard Shine?

It's rather a long story, but the short version is we were tired of searching for an organic beard oil that still smelled great and didn't cost a fortune. We even tried one of those "beard clubs" until we found out was was in it. Alas, we set out with one simple goal, create a beard oil that is organic and smells great. That's it. Enjoy.

Q: When will I get my order?

We will notify you when your order ships and you'll receive a tracking number. Since we're a small team, we only ship 2-3 days a week, so expect 1-2 days of handling and then 2-3 days to receive your order. If you have any other questions, just send us an email.

Q: What's in your oil?

All of our oils start with a blend of 100% organic Jojoba & Sweet Almond Oils, both of which are proven to keep your beard healthy while protecting and nourishing the skin underneath. Also, our oil is never tested on animals and is only tested on bearded men, although many of them might look like animals.

Q: Problem with your order?

First of all, we're sorry to hear that. Secondly, please email us at beardshine@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible (usually in less than a day).

Q: What can I do with your oil?

Ok, you're an adult, you know what this is for. Please don't dump it in your eye or eat it. This is for external use on your beard and hair only. We are not responsible in the event of misuse.
